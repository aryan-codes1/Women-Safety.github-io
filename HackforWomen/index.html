Problems faced

HEALTH AND SANITATION

Safe water access, health and sanitation are the building blocks for women .To solve the health and sanitation problem for women in rural area few steps should be taken"

Seprate Toilet Should be Provided

Due to the lack of clean, private, water and sanitation facilities women are unable to practice personal sanitary hygiene.Though poor sanitation leads to the spread of diseases like cholera and diarrhoea

Menstrual Hygine

This is the most common problem faced by women due to the lack of access to menstrual hygine products and facilities. Use of clean cloth and sanitary pads should be made in practice and should properly be disposed.

Seminar

Seminars should be conducted for raising the awareness among womens about health and sanitation .The role of mens towards women hygine mangement is another important aspect should not be neglected in ground of gender inequality.So,seminars and workshop in rural area should be provided.
